I'm Ellie, a second-year Applied Mathematics student at North Carolina State University with minors in Computer Programming and Science Communication. I've previously worked as a tutor in mathematics, English, and biology and am currently a teaching assistant for the Computer Science Department at NC State. Some of my interests include high-level programming, exoplanet research, and outdoor recreation and education.


I'm driven by the dream to work alongside those who develop new ways to gather and analyze data about space and our beautiful planet. I'm especially excited about integrating sustainable practices into the development of scientific instruments and research equipment that will shape future exploration of space and of the Earth.
